Kelly Lu, mononymously known by her SNS tag @kelly_sux, is a 27 year old female of Vietnamese descent. Kelly was born and raised in America after her family immigrated over. While growing up she was immersed in both Korean pop music and Japanese animation, which have not only influenced her interests, but also her art style. The movies Paprika and Akira, as well as the Japanese snack Milkey are good examples of how Japanese culture has influenced her. Throughout her life as a student she attended art focused schools in order to learn a variety of techniques, but ultimately her interest in Japanese culture and Japanese art styles is what influenced her the most. Kelly is an avid fan of Japanese horror, taking a lot of inspiration from various Japanese horror manga artists, and even living in Japan for 2 years post University graduation.
